Pricing and Transactions

Prices for the model will be determined after slicing (creating the render of the object) in our slicer program. Higher density and sized models will cost more. ALl models sold and their prices will be documented for future use to make pricing fair. Transactions are to happen in-person at the customers' choice of location, preferably the address listed in the form (this is mostly because we don't know how to code direct payments and shipping into our website). It should be noted that we may not be able to reach some locations. Lastly, although we wish against and do not desire to, we have the right to deny any customer our services for fair reasons.
