Pricing and Transactions

Prices for the model will be determined after slicing (creating the toolpath of the object) in our slicer program. Higher density and larger models will cost more. All models sold and their prices will be documented for future use to make pricing fair. Pickup will be arranged in a safe public place. (this is mostly because we don't know how to code direct payments and shipping into our website). It should be noted that we may not be able to reach some locations. Lastly, although we wish against and do not desire to, we have the right to deny any customer our services for fair reasons. We will also occasionally have in person sales. Location may vary.
